# Phase 17.3 — NLI fact-checking judge: A/B results & verdict

**Date:** 2026-06-19 · **Closes:** 17.3 (DEFERRED-039) + DEFERRED-031 (global-surface
faithfulness noise). **Design:** `docs/specs/2026-06-19-phase-17.3-nli-judge.md`.
**Model:** `cross-encoder/nli-deberta-v3-small` (CPU, labels contradiction/entailment/
neutral). **Archive under test:** the 14 global-surface gen rows of
`2026-06-17-…-v11-hybrid-tradition-b-…-full.json` (9 scorable; 5 are no-answer/empty).

## Verdict — NLI's contradiction-rate is the RIGHT fidelity signal for the global surface. RAGAS faithfulness was measuring the wrong thing there.

This is the first Phase-17 lever that **paid off** (CoVe / HyDE / RRF / semantic
chunking all came back neutral-to-negative). It fixes a real measurement defect on the
one surface that had it.

### Reading it

The gap rows are the classic DEFERRED-031 shape: *"The query 'X' surfaces lessons …
the common theme is Y."* RAGAS scores them **0.29–0.50** because the meta-claim
framing ("surfaces", "common theme") is not substring-recoverable from the contexts.

Three NLI policies, three different answers:

- **`nli_strict`** (entailment-only, ≈ RAGAS's intent) = **0.259** — *worse* than
  RAGAS. The meta-claims aren't entailed either. Useless; do not adopt.
- **`nli_contradiction_rate`** = **0.093** — the honest "describe what was retrieved"
  answers do **not contradict** their contexts. This is the clean discriminator: a
  real fabrication scores high contradiction (the Eiffel-Tower probe → 0.999; two rows
  here show genuine 0.25), while honest meta-claim answers score ~0.
- **`nli_faithfulness_lenient`** (= 1 − contradiction_rate) = **0.907** vs RAGAS 0.450
  — gives the global surface a sensible faithfulness *without* going blind to
  hallucination.

**So on the global surface, faithfulness should be measured as `1 − contradiction_rate`
(NLI), not RAGAS substring-recoverability.** That is the fix DEFERRED-031 needed: it
stops penalizing honest meta-claims while still catching contradictions.

### Honest limits (why this is a *good-enough*, not a *perfect*, fix)

- **Single model, smoke scale.** `nli-deberta-v3-small`, 9 scorable rows. Direction is
  clear; a 152-row / `-base`-model validation is future work if global gen-eval
  becomes a priority.
- **The `neutral` ambiguity is real but narrow.** NLI `neutral` covers both an honest
  meta-claim AND an *on-topic, unsupported* fabrication the context is silent on — the
  lenient policy can't catch the latter. But *off-topic* fabrications register as
  **contradiction** (Eiffel → 0.999), so the leak is limited to plausible-sounding,
  on-topic, unsupported claims. For the global surface — whose job is "describe what
  the substring search surfaced" — contradiction-rate is the meaningful signal anyway.
- **Sentence-split, not LLM statement extraction.** Coarser claim granularity than
  RAGAS; adequate for the short global answers.

## Decision

- **DEFERRED-031 RESOLVED (fixed).** Global-surface faithfulness defect is addressed:
  use NLI `1 − contradiction_rate` as the advisory faithfulness for that surface.
- **17.3 RESOLVED (built).** The NLI judge exists, is dockerized, integrated (TS
  client + metric family), and measured. It is **advisory / measurement-profile only**
  — production chat and the default baseline are unchanged.
- Other surfaces (lessons/code/chunks) keep RAGAS faithfulness — they never had this
  gap, and RAGAS works well there.
