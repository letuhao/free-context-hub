Delegation

The data boundary, made of rows. Every authority flows down from root as a scoped grant. Nothing grants upward or sideways.

Grant Capability Modal

Grant Capability

You can only grant within your own subtree — a capability you hold, at a scope you cover.

Within your subtree: you hold delegate @ global, which covers write @ project:free-context-hub. This grant is permitted.

Rejected — outside your subtree.

You tried to grant admin @ project:api-gateway but you hold no delegate/admin that covers api-gateway. You cannot grant upward or sideways.
